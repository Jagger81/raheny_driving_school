About Us.

Driving lessons Raheny provides skilled instructors in ensuring you are fully prepared to pass your test. Whether it is EDTs, pre test or need to gain confidence on the road, then we are the service for you. Our goal is to not only teach you how to drive but to feel confident while doing so!

He worked for F.C.F school of motoring, which was set up in 1967 by his father which was the first school of motoring in Co. Waterford. Jonathan has over 20 years experience teaching.
